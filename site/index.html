Local models. Real tools. Proof in the transcript.

LocalForge is an autonomous build harness for downloaded models. It gives Ollama or llama.cpp a controlled tool manifest, lets it work inside your machine, and records every model response, tool call, verification check, and final report.

Final reports are blocked until mutations are verified.

LocalForge classifies tool calls, tracks when a mutation succeeds, and refuses to accept a final success report until a concrete verification tool proves the postcondition.

01 inspect list_files {"path": "."}
02 mutate write_file projects/api/main.py
03 gate verification_required=true
04 prove python -m unittest discover -s tests
05 final run_final accepted
